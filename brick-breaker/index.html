Brick Breaker

Controls:

Use Left Arrow and Right Arrow, or A and D, to move the paddle

Press Up Arrow or W to launch the ball

Press Space or P to Pause/Unpause

Press Enter to restart the game

Info:

Completing a level increases the score multiplier by 1. Dropping the ball, however, drops the multiplier back down to 1.

__________________________

Level selector


Select the level you would like to play, then press Enter to refresh. (Choosing a level this way will reset your score and score multiplier)

__________________________

The 'easy' levels are the levels I used for testing. You can complete them by moving the paddle to the far left and launching the ball.
